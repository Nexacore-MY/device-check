How to take mirror photos
🪞 Stand in front of a mirror
📱 Screen photo: Hold your phone facing the mirror. Your front camera will capture the screen and front of the device in the reflection.
🔄 Back photo: Turn your phone around so the back faces the mirror. Your back camera will capture the back of the device in the reflection.
💡 Use good lighting. Make sure the full device is visible in the reflection, including edges and corners.
